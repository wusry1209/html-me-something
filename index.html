This is My Coding Story

My coding journey started several years ago. I solved a simple computer issue by searching google and I had been asked if I had an interest in coding.

After some convincing I began working with the beggining lessons in CodeAcademy and working with small projects with my friends that have web dev experience

Even with all my friends help I kept getting stuck on loops & functions & how they worked together. I became pretty discouraged and began drifting away from web development

I applied and was accepted to LaunchCode and now I am well on my way to becoming an all start developer!
